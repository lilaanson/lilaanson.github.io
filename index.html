LILA ANSON IS

a Boulder-based designer pursuing curation, website development, and graphic design. Her goal is to create spaces to encourage radical collaboration and discovery. Her most meaningful works have been through audience experience for large-scale events (like TEDxCU) and creating museum exhibits for children using Arduino. She has also designed visual material for festivals and magazines and coded many, many websites (including an entire capstone!).
